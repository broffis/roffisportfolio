About Me

Hello! My name is Brandon and I am learning to be a Junior Web Developer.

I began coding while in 8th grade, learning introductory Visual Basic. Through High School, I took a break from computers and began pursuing a career in the Culinary Arts. Over the next 8 years I honed my craft as a Chef, working through culinary school and up and down the East Coast.

After working in New England for 3 years I moved to North Carolina, eventually finding my way to Charlotte. Since 2015 I have worked at Red Ventures, a marketing and technology company based in Fort Mill, SC. Through hard work (and a little bit of luck) I have been able to quickly climb the ranks, going from agent to Workforce Manager to Operations Associate.

Currently, I am participating in the Internal Tech Accelarator (ITA) Program, learning a full stack of languages.
